Astrophysicist

I am a genderfluid astronomy postdoc at Texas A&M University studying supermassive black holes in local compact relic galaxies.

In addition to science, I am passionate about transforming academia and our society at large into a more equitable place. I am particularly interested in community-oriented programs supporting students from backgrounds that astronomy has historically excluded.
